About

A little about me and what I do

Hey, I'm Santiago — a UX/UI designer based in Medellín with a deep interest in how people think, behave, and interact with the products around them. That curiosity is what drew me to UX Research: I genuinely enjoy talking to people, understanding their needs, and translating those insights into design decisions that actually make sense.

I care about interfaces that feel effortless — clean, intentional, and free of unnecessary noise. Minimalism isn't just an aesthetic for me, it's a design principle: every element should earn its place.

I'm comfortable working in teams and enjoy the collaborative process of building something together. I'm also the kind of person who actively seeks out tools I've never used before — learning Twinmotion for architectural renders is a good example of that curiosity in action.
